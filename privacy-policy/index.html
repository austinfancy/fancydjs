Privacy Policy

Your information, handled carefully.

Effective July 12, 2026.

Information We Collect

When you reach out through our contact form or client portal, we collect the information you provide directly — your name, wedding date, venue, email, phone number, service interests, and any comments or planning details you share with us.

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to respond to your inquiry, prepare quotes and contracts, plan and perform your event, and communicate with you before, during, and after your wedding. We don’t use your information for anything beyond running our DJ/MC business and serving you as a client.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form and client portal are powered by Dubsado, a client-management platform. Our music pages embed playlists hosted on Spotify. These providers may process information under their own privacy policies when you interact with their embedded forms or players on our site.

Cookies & Tracking

We don’t use advertising trackers or third-party ad cookies on this site. Embedded content from Dubsado and Spotify may set their own cookies required for that content to function.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information you provide is used only to plan and deliver your event.

Data Security & Retention

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us and retain it only as long as necessary for business and legal purposes, such as record-keeping for booked events.

Your Rights

You can contact us at any time to ask what information we have on file, or to request a correction or deletion.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.

Contact Us
