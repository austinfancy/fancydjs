How It Works

From “hello” to last call.

A five-step process that starts the day you reach out and doesn’t end until the last songs played.

01

Contact Us

Reach out by phone, text, email, or our contact form. We’ll check your date and set up an initial consultation.

02

Book With Us

We hold your date for one week after we meet — no pressure. After the consultation we’ll send over your contract and set up your planning account so it’s ready the moment you decide to move forward.

03

Planning Begins

Use our online portal to build your celebration — music, timeline, every detail. We’ll stay in touch in the months leading up to your wedding, and check in 15 days before your wedding to schedule your final meeting.

04

The Final Meeting

We connect by phone or Zoom the week of your wedding to finalize music, timeline, and flow — and build your custom event outline.

05

The Big Day

We show up prepared, read the room, and keep the dance floor full from the first song to the last. You just enjoy the night.
